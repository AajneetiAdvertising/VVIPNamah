VVIP Namah: Premium Housing on NH 24 Highway, Ghaziabad Starting at ₹1.88 Cr

Check out VVIP Namah in NH 24 Highway, one of the upcoming under-construction housing societies in Ghaziabad. Brought to you by VVIP Spaces, this society offers apartments for sale and is scheduled for possession in May 2028. As a RERA-registered society, VVIP Namah ensures transparency and reliability, with all project details available on the state RERA portal under registration number UPRERAPRJ576907. Known for delivering quality real estate projects, VVIP Spaces has a reputation for excellence, having completed eight projects so far.

VVIP Namah boasts around 483 units with a variety of configurations to meet the diverse needs of homebuyers. The society offers exceptional amenities such as a swimming pool, gym, landscaped gardens, and a children's play area. Located near Columbia Asia Hospital, the project ensures easy access to quality healthcare. The NH 24 Highway locality is well-connected, with Maharauli Ghaziabad railway station being the nearest transit point. Additionally, the property prices in this area have seen significant growth, making VVIP Namah a promising investment.
